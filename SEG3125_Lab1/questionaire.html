A little bit about the game

Doki Doki Literature Club is a visual novel game. Therefore, its gameplay has low level of interactivity and consists of scenes with statis 2D images of characters in a first-person perspective. The website is the main page of the game. It offers a small video as descrition, quickly followed by the download link, a couple of words from Monika- the main character and some more niche information.
